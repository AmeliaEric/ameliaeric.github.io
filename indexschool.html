Author

I'm a Author that grew from school poetry days & competitions to 'Stars of Serendipity' my debut poetry collection"

My journey as an author culminates in the release of my debut poetry book, 'Stars of Serendipity.' The seeds of my passion for poetry were planted during school poetry reading days and a competitionin second grade, where I first stepped onto the stage to share my verses. Those early moments of connection and expression stayed with me, even as life led me down the paths of software development and full-time studies. Though poetry took a backseat for a while, its call remained undeniable. Returning to it later in life became a way to release the connections and thoughts that had accumulated over time. 'Stars of Serendipity' is a collection that reflects evolution of people I have met to become myself – from the youthful read alouds that sparked my love for poetry to the present, where I stand as an author, weaving intricate emotions into words. It's a testament to growth, resilience, and the enduring passion that has shaped my path.
